Floating Coffin

There's not too much in the way of experimentation on Floating Coffin, but some of the songs have a light, breezy euphoric feel while others drown you in a heavy psychedelic syrup.

It runs at an easy pace but it certainly doubles down on heaviness to make up for that, galloping on hypnotic grooves and bubbling bass-lines that drive each track forward, even the slow jams. Full of colour, sticky melodies and bizarre, sour flavour.
