Songs About Death and Dying Vol. 3

Mellow folky lo-fi tunes and evil twee pop on acid. While the guitar lines are similar in many of these songs they often take weird and surprising turns. This album has more developed and memorable songs than the first two OCS albums (Songs About Death and Dying Vol. 1 and Vol. 2), and sounds more similar to the first two albums released as Thee Oh Sees (Cool Death of Island Raiders, Sucks Blood). It's kind of long and some of the songs are better than others, but it's a consistently good release.

The perfect sandtrudtrack for a walk in the woods along the stream.
